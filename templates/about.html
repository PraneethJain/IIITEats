ABOUT

Our History:

IIITH Eats was founded in 2023 by a group of students with the goal of making it easier for IIITH students to enjoy great food from their favorite canteens without leaving campus. We know that sometimes you just don't have the time or energy to make the trek to your favorite canteen, and that's where we come in. With IIITH Eats, you can enjoy your favorite meals in the comfort of your own room, all while supporting your fellow students.

Our Partners:

We work with three specific canteens at IIIT Hyderabad(Vindhya Canteen, Tantra Canteen, BasketBall Court Canteen) to offer you a diverse range of cuisines and flavors. We're proud to support these canteens and help them reach more customers through our delivery service. By partnering with IIITH Eats, the canteens can focus on what they do best - making delicious food - while we take care of the delivery logistics.

Fast and Reliable:

We know that when you're hungry, you want your food fast. That's why we strive to provide the fastest and most reliable delivery service possible. Our delivery team is made up of highly organized and efficient student volunteers who are committed to getting your food to you as quickly as possible. We use the latest technology to coordinate deliveries and ensure that everything runs smoothly.

Contactless Delivery:

We're committed to your health and safety. That's why we offer contactless delivery options to minimize contact between our delivery team and customers. Our student volunteers are trained in safe handling practices and take all necessary precautions to ensure your food is delivered safely.
